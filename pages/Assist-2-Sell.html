Assist-2-Sell, Inc. is a Nevada, USA corporation located at 1610 Meadow Wood Lane, Reno, NV 89502. The company is a franchisor of real estate brokerage offices that offer services in residential home sales. They utilize a flat rate commission structure and are considered a discount real estate company. The company was started by Mary LaMeres-Pomin and Lyle E. Martin in 1987. There are approx. 450 real estate franchise offices in 44 U.S. states and five Canadian provinces as of January 2009. Assist-2-Sell is based in Reno , Nevada and continues to be run by LaMeres-Pomin and Martin.
