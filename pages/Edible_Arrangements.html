Edible Arrangements

Edible Arrangements is a U.S. -based franchising business that specializes in fresh fruit arrangements, melding the concept of fruit baskets with designs inspired by the floral business. [1] The company also sells a variety of specialty fruit gift items, such as gift boxes featuring premium chocolate dipped fruit, and fresh-fruit-to-go products.

Edible Arrangements is a U.S. -based franchising business that specializes in fresh fruit arrangements, melding the concept of fruit baskets with design inspired by the floral business. [1] The company also sells a variety of specialty fruit gift items such as gift boxes featuring premium chocolate dipped fruit. In 2010, the company expanded the scope of its goods to include fresh fruit to-go products for customers searching for a healthy snack alternative. Some of the company's offerings in this category include fruit smoothies, fruit salads, fruit parfaits and fruit pops that are available at many of the company's franchisee locations.
