Jersey Mike's Subs

Jersey Mike's Subs is an American submarine sandwich chain headquartered in Manasquan, New Jersey . [1] The Jersey Mike's franchise has almost 1,300 locations open and about 70 more in development across the United States. Five additional locations are open outside the U.S. [2]
