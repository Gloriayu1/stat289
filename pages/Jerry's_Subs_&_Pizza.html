Jerry's Subs & Pizza is a fast casual sandwich and pizza restaurant chain in the Washington, DC area and currently has over 140 locations. [1] Jerry's was founded in 1954 in Wheaton, Maryland [2] and is headquartered in Gaithersburg, Maryland . [3] The chain is known for its advertising campaign with commercials featuring actors impersonating politicians , such as Bill Clinton and George Bush .
