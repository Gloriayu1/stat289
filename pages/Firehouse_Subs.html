Firehouse Subs

Firehouse Subs ( Firehouse of America, LLC ) is a U.S. -based, fast casual restaurant chain founded in 1994 in Jacksonville , Florida by former firefighter brothers Robin and Chris Sorensen. [1] Firehouse Subs has opened over 1,135 franchise restaurants in 44 states as well as Puerto Rico and Canada .

Firehouse Subs , is a US-based, fast casual restaurant chain that specializes in hot subs . Founded during 1994 in Jacksonville, Florida by former firefighting brothers Robin and Chris Sorensen, Firehouse Subs serves sandwiches with 4 oz or 8oz portions of meats and cheeses, steamed hot and placed atop fresh produce on a toasted sub roll. Locations offer a family-oriented atmosphere, with a firefighter theme that includes fire equipment throughout the store and a menu that features sandwich names such as Engineer, Hook & Ladder and Engine Company, with the option of having your sub "fully involved" (Mayo, Mustard, Lettuce, Tomato, Onion and a Dill Spear on the side). Pictures mounted on the walls highlight local firefighting history, as well as the founder's history of firefighting service.
