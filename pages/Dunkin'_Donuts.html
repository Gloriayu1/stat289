Dunkin' Donuts

Dunkin' Donuts , rebranding as Dunkin' (and also known colloquially as Dunkies , Dunkins , and Dunk [3] ) is an American multinational quick service restaurant chain based in Canton, Massachusetts . It was founded in 1950 by William Rosenberg in Quincy, Massachusetts , and is one of the largest coffee and baked goods chains in the world, with more than 12,000 restaurants in 36 countries. [4] [5] The chain's products include donuts , bagels , other baked goods, and a variety of hot and iced beverages. [6] The chain is currently owned by Dunkin' Brands .
