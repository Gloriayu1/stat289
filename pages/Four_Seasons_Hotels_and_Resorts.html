Four Seasons Hotels and Resorts

Four Seasons Hotels Limited , trading as Four Seasons Hotels and Resorts , is an international luxury hospitality company [2] headquartered in Toronto, Ontario . [3] Four Seasons operates more than 100 hotels worldwide. [4] Since 2007, Bill Gates (through Cascade Investment ) and Prince Al-Waleed bin Talal have been majority owners of the company. [5]
