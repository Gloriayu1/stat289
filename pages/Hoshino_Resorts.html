Hoshino Resorts

Hoshino Resort Co., Ltd. ( 株式会社 星野リゾート , Kabushiki Kaisha Hoshino Risōto ) is a Japan-based international operator of ryokan (Japanese inns) with its head office in Karuizawa, Nagano . [1] Founded in 1904 by Kuniji Hoshino as a forestry business in Karuizawa, [2] in the Japanese Alps , it opened its first hot spring resort in 1914.
