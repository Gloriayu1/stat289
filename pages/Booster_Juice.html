Booster Juice

BoosterJuice Ltd. is Canada 's largest chain of fresh juice and smoothie bars, owned and led by Dale Wishewan , President and CEO. The chain specializes in smoothies made of pure juice , fruit sorbet or vanilla frozen yogurt , frozen fruit, fresh yogurt and ice. Founded in 1999 in Sherwood Park , Alberta, this company was quickly franchised, setting a Canadian record for opening 50 stores in the first 2 years. Since then, it has grown to have more than 350 traditional and 25 non-traditional locations in Canada , 1 in India , 1 in Dubai , 2 in the United States and 3 in Mexico .
