Jimmy John's

Jimmy John's Franchise, LLC is an American franchised sandwich restaurant chain, specializing in delivery . It was founded by Jimmy John Liautaud in 1983 and is headquartered in Champaign , Illinois . In 35 years, the company has grown to more than 2,800 locations [3] [1] in all states except Alaska, Connecticut, Hawaii, Maine, New Hampshire, Rhode Island and Vermont. [4] Jimmy John's has opened approximately 200 locations per year over the past three years. [5] As of 2014 [update] , 98% of the locations are franchise -owned. [6]
