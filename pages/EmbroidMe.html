EmbroidMe

EmbroidMe is a U.S based franchise that specializes in custom embroidery and screen printing . [1] It is part of the United Franchise Group . The company was founded in 2000 by Ray Titus who launched the first store in West Palm Beach . [2] EmbroidMe has over 250 units across the world, with regional offices in the United States and Australia . Other locations include Bahamas , Canada , England , Mexico , New Zealand , Nicaragua , South Africa and Trinadad and Tobago .
