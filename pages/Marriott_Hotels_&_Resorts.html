Marriott Hotels & Resorts

Marriott Hotels & Resorts is Marriott International 's flagship brand of full-service hotels and resorts . The company, based in Bethesda, Maryland , is repeatedly included on the Forbes list of best companies to work for, and it was voted the fourth best company to work for in the UK by The Times in 2009.
