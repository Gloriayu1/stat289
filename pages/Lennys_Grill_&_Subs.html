Lenny's Sub Shop is a Philadelphia-style sub shop which has franchises located across the country. It was founded in Bartlett, TN in 1998. Lenny's is known for their Philly cheesesteaks and hand-sliced subs. They serve fourteen cold subs, four hot subs, five salads, and four kids' meals. The company's headquarters is located in Memphis, Tennessee. Lenny's regular size subs are a 7 1/2 inches long with a 1/2 pound of meat and cheese, while their larges are 15 inches long with a full pound of meat and cheese.
