7 Days Inn

7 Days Group Holdings Limited [1] ( NYSE : SVN), operating as 7 Days Inn ( Chinese : 7天酒店 ; pinyin : Qītiān Jiǔdiàn ; Jyutping : Cat1 tin1 zau2 dim3 ), is a Chinese budget hotel chain. It was established in 2005. [2] It has its headquarters in the Creative Industry Zone [note 1] in Haizhu District , Guangzhou . [3] [4]
