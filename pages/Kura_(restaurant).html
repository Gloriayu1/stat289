Kura (restaurant)

Kura zushi is a sushi restaurant chain located in Japan . [1] [2] It has 362 locations in Japan, and a few more outside Japan. While it is a conveyor belt sushi chain, it relies on a high level of automation allowing the average location to function with fifteen to twenty staff members. [3]
